About the Client

This fast growing restaurant serves 15 locations nationwide and needed a modern site inline with their brand that could serve up detailed menus - all customized per location. As the lead developer, I custom built this responsive WordPress theme using GeoLocation to serve up the menus closest to the user. The site also utilizes custom user roles to allow the “manager” of each location to update their localized menus and specials on a daily basis.
