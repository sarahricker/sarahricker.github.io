What I Do?

I’m a full stack web developer working out of Austin, Texas. I help small to medium-size businesses clarify their brand and engage with their customer base by creating functional, yet creative interactive digital experiences. I've helped over 200+ clients find their voice and present their brand in engaging ways that maintains their brand identity and increases visibility to their services and products.

As a developer, I adhere to the concept that tech, as much as design, is an artform and can be both beautiful and inspiring. I believe in better living through automation, agile and lean development practices, and an ever open mindset to how new technologies can enhance both personal and business life.

When I’m not developing, you can find me enjoying (and creating) music, painting/sculpting/otherwise making an artistic mess of my home, or head-down in a great novel.
