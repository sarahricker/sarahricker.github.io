About the Client

Little Dragon are a Swedish electronic music band from Gothenburg. Their latest record , Nabuma Rubberband, has propelled the band to new heights as they continue touring the world playing to large crowds.

Workhorse Digital designed and developed a website to support their latest release. While fairly straight forward in functionality, our focus was more on a custom design and experience that fit Little Dragon’s brand. The new responsive website features a simple CMS allowing the band to update upcoming shows and other information.
