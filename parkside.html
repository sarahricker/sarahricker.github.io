About the Client

In the fall of 1991, Parkside Community School opened with 75 children. For 23 years, their school has flourished as one of Austin’s most beloved, growing enrollment to 135 students. This new website for Parkside Community Montessori School helped further support the school’s transition into a beautiful new 12-acre campus in South Austin, Tx, after more than 23 years close to downtown.

In addition to a new online presence and re-strategized content architecture, the new website features a sync-ready calendar, online payment capability, tour scheduling and more. The website also helps drive visitors to make donations toward their Capital Campaign which supports their new permanent home.
